    Sản phẩm

    On television, there are many kinds of programs. Some are good, like cartoons, educational shows, and news for kids. But some programs are not suitable for children. These programs may include: • Violence: fighting, killing, or hurting others. • Bad language: rude words, swearing, or impolite expressions. • Scary or horror scenes: monsters, ghosts, or dangerous actions. • Inappropriate behaviors: stealing, lying, or cheating. These contents are harmful for children because they are still too young to know what is right or wrong. 2️⃣ How does this affect children? When children watch violent or bad programs, they may: • Learn bad habits • Copy bad actions • Use bad words • Think violence is normal For example: • After watching many action movies, some children like to pretend to fight with their friends. They may hit, push, or kick others without understanding that it’s dangerous. • Or, when children hear bad words on TV, they may repeat those words at school or at home. This makes them rude and disrespectful. According to psychologists, young children easily imitate what they see and hear. TV programs with bad content can change their behavior, thinking, and personality over time.
